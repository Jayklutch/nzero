Sustainability Trust - Wellington

What is it, how does it work?

The Outline

The Sustainability Trust wanted some of the electricity created by their solar panels to be made available for vehicle charging. It needed to include the ability to charge as many different types of vehicles as possible and also have other uses if possible

The Result

With an integrated charge controller on one side, with the addition of the correct cable, almost every OEM EV can be charged from this outlet.

Caravan sockets and HPM sockets on the other side allow for food trucks plugging in.
